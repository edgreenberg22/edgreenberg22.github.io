How do I organize my receipts?

Take a photo of your receipt within the app. Smart Envelope AI will automatically scan and categorize it.

How do subscriptions work?

The AI receipt scanning feature requires an AI Tier subscription ($4.99/month). A 14-day free trial is available. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, select Smart Envelope AI, tap Cancel Subscription.

Trouble with the subscribe button?

Make sure you are signed in to your Apple ID, check your internet connection, force-close and reopen the app, or restart your device. If the issue persists, email us.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

Smart Envelope AI is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Smart Envelope AI does not store your personal data on any external servers. All receipt data, merchant names, and envelope totals are stored locally on your device or within your private iCloud storage.

2. Processing (AI)

When you use the AI features to scan a receipt, the data is sent securely to the Anthropic API for text extraction. This data is not used to train models and is not shared with third parties.

3. Subscriptions

All subscription payments and renewals are handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card or billing information.

4. Contact Us

For questions regarding your privacy: edgreenberg22@gmail.com
