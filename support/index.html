Frequently Asked Questions

How do I organize my receipts?

Simply take a photo of your receipt within the app. Smart Envelope AI will automatically scan and categorize it for you.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your receipt data is stored locally on your device. No account or login is required to use the app. For details, see our Privacy Policy.

How do subscriptions work?

The AI receipt scanning feature requires an AI Tier subscription ($4.99/month). A 14-day free trial is available. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID and can be cancelled anytime from your device's Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Smart Envelope AI
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

I'm having trouble with the subscribe button.

If the subscribe button is not responding, please try the following:

  1. Make sure you're signed in to your Apple ID
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Force-close the app and reopen it
  4. Restart your device

If the issue persists, please email us at edgreenberg22@gmail.com with details.
